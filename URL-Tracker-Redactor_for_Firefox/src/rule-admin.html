introduction

Web cookies have been uses to track users for a long time. Lately they have come in for so unfavorable treatment and the data harvesting industry has moved on to new techniques. Embeddeding the tracking values directly into the links you click. This has the added advantage of also remaining in your bookmarks. Removing cookies is of no use at all.

A prominent vendor of this tracking technolgy is Google Analytics
This is a example of a URL seeded by tag values used by their tools.
https://buffer.com/email-courses/actionable-social-media-strategies/?utm_destination=buffer&utm_medium=post-original&utm_content=-image&utm_campaign=25-social-media-strategie
Have a quick look through you news feed and you will see plenty of examples.


The parameters are
utm_destination
utm_medium
utm_content
utm_campaign
utm_term
utm_offer

how it works

The add-on examins all outbound links are applied rewriting rules to them to remove these fields

what do you do

Not much. Just install the add-on and carry on as before.

your options

The suitable rules for rewriting URLs comes with the addon. But you can change them to any extent, and make your own.

policies

Policies are grouped according to source, on what page the links are found. but the rewriting applies to the where the link points to, not where it is from.

Everywhere Policies

Policy applies to all pages everywhere.

Domain-based Policies

Policy applies to all pages within a whole domain such as "google.com"

Hostname Policies

Policy applies to all pages with a given hosname "https://mail.google.com"

URL/Page Policies

Policy applies to particular URLs, such as "https://www.google.fi/maps"

defaults

refresh

Refresh the live policies

backup

Make a backup of all policies.
